## Step 1: Gather Materials

You'll need these items:

| Item | Quantity | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| Furnace | 1 | Base block for frying pan |
| Iron Pressure Plate | 1 | The "pan" surface |
| Egg | 1-3 | Your first ingredient! |

{% hint style="info" %}
**Don't have eggs?** Find chickens nearby, or use `/hl give FRIED_EGG` if you're an operator to test!
{% endhint %}

***

## Step 2: Build the Frying Pan

1. **Place a Furnace** on the ground
2. **Place the Iron Pressure Plate** (Heavy Weighted Pressure Plate) on top of the furnace

![Frying Pan Setup](../.gitbook/assets/frying-pan-setup.png)

That's it! When built correctly, the pressure plate becomes a functional cooking surface.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Pro Tip:** The furnace doesn't need fuel! The Frying Pan is a magical cooking station that works instantly.
{% endhint %}

***

## Step 3: Start Cooking!

1. **Right-click** the Iron Pressure Plate (your Frying Pan)
2. A cooking GUI will open
3. **Place 1-3 eggs** in the ingredient slots
4. Watch the cooking progress bar fill up
5. **Take your Fried Eggs** from the output slot!
